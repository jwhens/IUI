Jasper Wright Hensley

Born in Beech Grove, Indiana, Jasper Wright Hensley is the sort of person who believes that they might one day be somebody, but not quite yet. Despite being a writer and creating worlds and characters who go on fabulous adventures and conquer many trials, Jasper is not typically the type of person who enjoys talking about himself very much and does not believes himself to be a very interesting person. This introductory paragraph needs to have a lot of text on it in order to make it seem like the main body, so he's going to keep typing here for a while, and he appreciates your patience if you do so happen to read it.
Jasper was born and raised in the Indianapolis metropolitan area and never saw the ocean until they were eighteen years old. He's convinced that's a pretty common experience for people who are from the middle of nowhere and don't travel much, now that he's been a lot of places and met a lot of people. Most people never go hardly anywhere or see much of anything, and he hopes he can bring a piece of the world to other people, if only for the briefest of moments. Maybe that's what this tiny website can begin to do, if he gets better at writing them.

More About Our Boy


So, our guy, Jasper, they like to read. A little too much, if believe people they know. Sometimes, that makes Jasper think that he knows some things, which is a common error. Lots of people read books and it makes them believe that they're now wise. And then they go back to their hometowns and get told they've outgrown their britches, higher than the corn and bigger than their raisings. So, because of that and many other factors, Jasper doesn't live in Indiana anymore if he can help it, though he does visit from time to time. After all, his family's roots are planted there awful deep.
